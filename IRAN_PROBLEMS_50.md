# Top 50 Buildable Problems in Iran

Organized by impact + feasibility. Each will have 3-5 "bite-sized" projects.

## üíß WATER (10 problems - CRITICAL)

### P1: Qanat System Collapsing Nationwide
**Location:** Nationwide | **Severity:** Critical
- 37,000 km of ancient underground water channels deteriorating
- Provides water for millions, UNESCO World Heritage at risk
- **Projects:** Restoration pilots, mapping, training, IoT monitoring, heritage sites

### P2: Groundwater Depletion Crisis  
**Location:** Central Iran | **Severity:** Critical
- Aquifers depleting 70% faster than recharge rate
- Wells running dry, 70% of Iran's water from groundwater
- **Projects:** Aquifer recharge pilots, monitoring networks, well metering, recharge studies

### P3: Zayandeh Rud River Completely Dried
**Location:** Isfahan | **Severity:** Critical
- Historic river dry for months annually, 3M+ affected
- 400-year-old bridges over dry riverbeds, agriculture collapsing
- **Projects:** Wetland restoration, flow management, treated wastewater reuse

### P4: Lake Urmia Shrinking - Salt Desert Growing
**Location:** Urmia | **Severity:** Critical
- Lost 90% of volume, creating salt storms affecting 16M people
- Ecosystem collapse, was 6th largest saltwater lake
- **Projects:** Wetland restoration, tree barriers, water allocation reform

### P5: Urban Water Leakage - 30% Lost in Distribution
**Location:** Tehran + major cities | **Severity:** High
- Century-old pipes leak 30% of supply, daily water rationing
- Aging infrastructure costs billions annually
- **Projects:** Pipe replacement pilots, leak detection sensors, smart meters

### P6: Agricultural Water Waste - Flood Irrigation
**Location:** Nationwide | **Severity:** Critical
- 90% of water to agriculture, 60% wasted
- Farmers losing 40% of crops to drought
- **Projects:** Drip irrigation pilots, farmer training, efficient tech subsidies

### P7: Unsustainable Dam Construction
**Location:** Nationwide | **Severity:** High
- 600+ dams built without ecological studies
- Rivers diverted, downstream communities losing water
- **Projects:** Environmental impact studies, dam removal pilots, alternative solutions

### P8: Saltwater Intrusion - Coastal Aquifers
**Location:** Bushehr, Persian Gulf coast | **Severity:** High
- Over-pumping lets seawater contaminate wells
- Agriculture threatened, wells becoming unusable
- **Projects:** Pumping regulation, desalination plants, recharge barriers

### P9: Permanent Water Rationing in Cities
**Location:** Multiple cities | **Severity:** High
- Rotational shutoffs 8-12 hours daily, hospitals/schools affected
- **Projects:** Storage tanks, pressure management, efficiency programs

### P10: No Rainwater Harvesting
**Location:** Nationwide | **Severity:** Medium
- Heavy rains flood then run off unused
- No systems to capture/store for dry months
- **Projects:** Rooftop systems, community cisterns, infiltration basins, school pilots
